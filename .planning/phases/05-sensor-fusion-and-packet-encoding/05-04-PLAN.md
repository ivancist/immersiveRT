<objective>
Build the dual orientation pipeline (D-03): the OS-fused PRIMARY path (`eulerToQuat`, W3C Z-X-Y) and
the Madgwick SECONDARY path (`updateMadgwick` via the `ahrs` package, with the runtime-configurable
beta ramp of SENS-02). Written test-first because the quaternion conversion and unit handling are
exact math with known-answer cases, and the unit-conversion pitfalls (deg/s vs rad/s, m/s² vs g) are
the single most common source of silent orientation bugs.

Purpose: Deliver SENS-01 (Madgwick on-device) and SENS-02 (beta ramp) as isolated, unit-tested
functions, plus the OS-fused primary quaternion that Plan 06 uses as the default packet orientation.

</objective>

## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** phone player in an active session, **I want to** have my phone's motion and touch fused
on-device (OS-fused orientation, dead-reckoned position, ZUPT-gated gesture displacement) and
streamed to every desktop as a compact 36-byte binary packet at my device's maximum sample rate,
**so that** each desktop can render my movements with low-latency, drift-honest fidelity.

> This plan delivers the orientation fusion — both the OS-fused primary quaternion and the Madgwick
> secondary — that becomes the packet's `qw/qx/qy/qz` in Plan 06.
