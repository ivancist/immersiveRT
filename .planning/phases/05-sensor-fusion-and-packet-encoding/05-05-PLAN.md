## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** phone player in an active session, **I want to** have my phone's motion and touch fused
on-device (OS-fused orientation, dead-reckoned position, ZUPT-gated gesture displacement) and
streamed to every desktop as a compact 36-byte binary packet at my device's maximum sample rate,
**so that** each desktop can render my movements with low-latency, drift-honest fidelity.

> This plan delivers the dead-reckoning position + drift-confidence engine that fills the packet's
> `px/py/pz` and `driftConfidence` fields (wired in Plan 07).

  <behavior>
    ZUPTDetector (zupt.test.ts):
    - partial window: fewer than 5 samples pushed ŌåÆ update() returns false regardless of variance.
    - still detection: after pushing Ōēź5 low-variance samples spanning Ōēź300ms of timestamps (e.g. constant magnitude 9.81 at t = 0,60,120,...,300), update() returns true.
    - motion rejection: pushing high-variance samples (alternating 5 and 15) over the window returns false (variance > adaptiveThreshold).
    - window eviction: samples older than windowMs (300) are dropped ŌĆö after feeding still samples then a burst of moving samples spanning >300ms, the detector reflects only the recent window.
    - adaptive threshold: setting detector.adaptiveThreshold to a larger value makes a previously-moving window read as still (threshold is runtime-configurable, SENS-03).

    Kalman1D (kalman.test.ts):
    - integration: starting at rest, predict(1.0 /*m/s┬▓*/, 0.1 /*s*/) called repeatedly increases the returned position monotonically (velocity accumulates then position accumulates).
    - reset: after some predict() calls build up velocity, resetVelocity() makes the next predict(0, dt) leave position unchanged (velocity is zero) and reduces covariance P.
    - confidence range: driftConfidence() always returns a value in [0, 1].
    - confidence behavior: driftConfidence() is at/near its max right after resetVelocity() and decreases (or stays Ōēż previous) as more predict() calls grow P without a reset.
    - NaN guard: predict(NaN, 0.1) does not turn the internal position/velocity into NaN (guarded).
  </behavior>
