| ARKit camera → app | Camera frames and VIO pose enter app process; only motion/pose may leave (D-14) |
| app → wire (WebRTC) | Encoded 36-byte packets cross to desktop peers over the unreliable data channel |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-06.3-01 | Tampering | ARKit pose floats into SensorPacket | medium | mitigate | `safeFloat()` guards every float write in `SensorPacketEncoder.encodePacket` (existing V5 control); NaN/Inf poses become 0, never corrupt wire bytes. |
| T-06.3-02 | Information Disclosure | ARFrame raw camera/depth buffers | high | mitigate | D-14 enforced by construction: headless ARSession, no rendering view; only `camera.transform`/`trackingState` read. Grep gate in Task 1 acceptance asserts zero raw-buffer property access in app sources (blocks on high per config). |
